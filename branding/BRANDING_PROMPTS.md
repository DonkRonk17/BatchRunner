## Visual Style Guide

**Brand Colors:**
- Primary: Dark Navy (#0a0e27)
- Accent: Bright Cyan (#00d4ff)
- Supporting: Deep Purple (#1a0d2e), Teal (#00fff5)
- Text: White (#ffffff), Light Gray (#e0e0e0)

**Typography:**
- Modern sans-serif (Roboto, Inter, SF Pro)
- Bold weights for titles
- Clean, readable

**Visual Elements:**
- Lightning bolts (speed, execution)
- Parallel arrows (parallel execution)
- Convergence patterns (dependency management)
- Circuit board motifs (automation)
- Minimal gradients (subtle when used)

**Mood:**
- Professional
- Fast and efficient
- Reliable automation
- Intelligent orchestration
- Part of unified Team Brain ecosystem
